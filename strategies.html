Scalping

Short-term trades aiming for small profits per trade. Requires fast execution.

Momentum Trading

Capitalizes on stocks moving strongly in one direction. Focuses on news-driven moves or breakout patterns.

Swing Trading

Trades lasting several days to capture short-term trends. Uses technical indicators to time entries/exits.
