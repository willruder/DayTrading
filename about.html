Introduction

Day trading is buying and selling financial instruments within a single trading day. Traders aim to capitalize on small price movements in highly liquid stocks or other assets.

Risks and Rewards

  • High potential profit, but high risk of loss
  • Requires discipline, speed, and proper strategy
  • Emphasizes capital preservation

Tools You Need

  • Real-time market data feeds
  • Charting software
  • Trading platform / broker account
  • News & research sources
